June 2025 - During this month I undertook a job simulation with Lloyds bank. One of my tasks was to redesign their mortgage calculator into a new and improved version. I took into account previous feedback from users about what was wrong with the old version and implemented new improvements and features into my new version. For example I created a pie chart to help visually display how the mortgage payments would be broken up. I used known formulas to calculate the payments using things like interest rates, down payments etc.
